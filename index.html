Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide high-quality secondary education that nurtures academic excellence, discipline, and moral values. Our vision is to become a leading private secondary school recognized for outstanding results and well-rounded graduates.

Our History

Established in 2010, Sunrise Secondary School was founded with a clear goal: to offer a safe, disciplined, and academically strong learning environment. Over the years, we have grown steadily while maintaining our commitment to quality education.

  • Passport size photos
